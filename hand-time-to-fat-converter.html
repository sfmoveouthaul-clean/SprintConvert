Benchmark note

This page does not invent extra timing-based benchmark tables. It only uses the same limited source-backed benchmark rules as the rest of the site.

When +0.24 helps most

The +0.24 estimate is most familiar in track-style hand-to-FAT discussion. Outside that context, system differences can be wider or simply different.

What not to do

Do not treat a hand-time adjustment as proof that two marks from different systems are truly equal.

Benchmark status

No general 100m benchmark is published in v1. The page still follows the same rule of showing a benchmark only where a supported source-backed match exists.

Why 100m still matters

A 100m time captures more of the athlete's full sprint profile than a short acceleration test alone, especially the balance between acceleration and speed endurance.

Why 100m still has limits

Wind, start consistency, track surface, and timing setup all change the result. Even FAT marks should be treated as context, not a total athlete evaluation.

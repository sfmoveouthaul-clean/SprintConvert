Privacy

Privacy policy for v1

This MVP is intentionally minimal. Calculations run in the browser, no account is required, and there is no email capture in v1.

What we do not collect in v1

  • No full names
  • No school names
  • No emails
  • No health or injury data
  • No exact birthdays
  • No precise location data

How the site works

Calculations run in the browser on your device. No account is required. There is no login and no payment processing in v1.

We do not sell personal information.

Minors and age ranges

This site uses broad age ranges only. It does not ask for an exact age or exact birthday.

This site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Future analytics

If analytics are added later, this privacy policy must be updated first.

Future email capture

If email capture is ever added later, it must be optional and useful, and this privacy policy must be updated first.

Contact

If you have a privacy concern, use the contact details on the contact page.

Why 40s can mislead

A 40-yard dash still depends on start quality, footwear, surface, and timing method. Two athletes can reach the same final time with different acceleration profiles.

When split data helps most

If you have a 10-yard or 20-yard split, the calculator can separate first-step quality from later speed more cleanly.

FAQ

40-yard questions

What makes a 40-yard dash estimate less reliable?

Hand timing, rolling or flying starts, missing splits, and trying to compare very different testing setups all reduce reliability.

Does a 40-yard time predict football success by itself?

No. It is just one testing input and should not be used alone to judge football performance, recruiting potential, or roster decisions.

Benchmark note

Flying sprints do not have a benchmark system in v1. Any benchmark card shown here is still tied to the limited supported distance/profile dataset.

Why coaches use flying sprints

They help isolate upright speed and can show whether an athlete has enough top-end ability to carry speed later in a sprint.

Why coaches should stay cautious

Flying sprint setup distance, timing gates, and the entry speed into the zone all change the result.
